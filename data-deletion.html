Data Deletion Request

Last updated: March 2025

BotMatic respects your privacy and complies with Meta Platform Terms. If you have connected your Instagram or Facebook account to BotMatic and wish to have your data deleted, you can request this at any time.

What data we store

  • Instagram / Facebook user ID and username
  • Access tokens for sending/receiving messages
  • Conversation history with the chatbot
  • Webhook subscription data

How to request data deletion

Send an email to our privacy team with subject "Data Deletion Request":

📧 privacy@botmatic.be

Include the following in your request:

  • Your Instagram or Facebook username
  • The email address associated with your account

What happens after your request

  • We will confirm receipt within 48 hours
  • All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Automatic deletion via Facebook

You can also disconnect BotMatic directly from your Facebook settings:

  • Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  • Find BotMatic and click Remove

When you remove the app via Facebook, we automatically receive a deletion callback and will remove your data within 30 days.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions: privacy@botmatic.be
