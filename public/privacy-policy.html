Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Who we are

We are BotMatic ("BotMatic", "we", "us"). We help businesses manage incoming conversations on channels connected through Meta services.

Data we receive from Meta

  • Meta user ID and account/page ID.
  • Username or profile name.
  • Message content sent to connected business channels.
  • Webhook events (for example: message received, message status, delivery/read events).

Why we use this data

  • To connect and operate business channels.
  • To process incoming conversations and send automated replies.
  • To keep services secure and monitor technical performance.

How to delete your data

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing info@botmatic.be.

  • Subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  • Include your channel details (for example username, phone, or page name).
  • We will confirm and process deletion within a reasonable period.

Detailed deletion process: www.botmatic.be/data-deletion

Questions about privacy?

Contact us at info@botmatic.be and we will help you quickly.
