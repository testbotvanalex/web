Data Deletion

Request deletion of your data handled by BotMatic.

How to request deletion

Send your request to info@botmatic.be with subject: Data Deletion Request.

Please include

  • Your name and contact email.
  • Your Meta username, page name, phone number, or other channel identifier.
  • Any additional details to help us identify your records.

What happens next

  • We confirm receipt of your request.
  • We verify and remove relevant personal data from active systems.
  • We send you a confirmation when deletion is completed.

Timing

We process deletion requests as soon as possible, typically within 30 days, unless legal obligations require a longer retention period.
